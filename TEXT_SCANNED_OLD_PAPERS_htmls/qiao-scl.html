Abstract This paper studies controllability properties of recurrent neural networks. The new contributions are: (1) an extension of a previous result to a slightly different model, (2) a formulation and proof of a necessary and sufficient condition, and (3) an analysis of a low-dimensional case for which the hypotheses made in previous work do not apply.
