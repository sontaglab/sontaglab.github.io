On Split Realizations of Response Maps over Rings
EDUARDO D. SONTAG
Department of Mathematics, Rutgers University, New Brunswick, New Jersey 08903
This paper deals with observability properties of realizations of linear response
maps defined over commutative rings. A characterization is given for those
maps which admit realizations which are simultaneously reachable and observable
in a strong sense. Applications are given to delay-differential systems.
